Game Features

Experience the Ultimate Combat Simulation

Massive Multiplayer Battles

Experience all-out warfare with up to 128 players fighting simultaneously across sprawling battlefields. Coordinate with your squad, communicate with your team, and dominate the opposition in the most epic multiplayer battles ever created.

  • 128 player battles on next-gen platforms
  • Dynamic squad system with voice communication
  • Real-time tactical map and objective markers
  • Cross-play support across all platforms

Destructible Environments

Nothing is safe from destruction. Blast through buildings, create new pathways, and reshape the battlefield to your tactical advantage. Our advanced destruction physics make every battle unique and unpredictable.

  • Fully destructible buildings and structures
  • Dynamic weather system affecting gameplay
  • Real-time environmental changes
  • Tactical destruction for strategic advantages

Advanced Arsenal

Command an extensive arsenal of modern military equipment. From assault rifles to rocket launchers, tanks to fighter jets, master over 100 weapons and vehicles to dominate land, air, and sea combat.

  • 70+ authentic weapons with full customization
  • 30+ military vehicles including tanks, helicopters, and jets
  • Advanced attachment system for weapons
  • Realistic ballistics and physics simulation

Deep Customization

Create your perfect soldier with extensive customization options. Personalize your character's appearance, loadout, and playstyle to match your tactical preferences and stand out on the battlefield.

  • Hundreds of character cosmetic options
  • Weapon skin and camo system
  • Vehicle customization and paint jobs
  • Player card and emblem creator

Game Modes

Conquest

The classic large-scale game mode. Capture and hold objectives across massive maps while managing team resources and respawn tickets.

Breakthrough

Attackers must capture sectors while defenders hold the line. Intense, focused combat with clear objectives and epic pushes.

Rush

Fast-paced combat where attackers destroy M-COM stations while defenders prevent the destruction of their objectives.

Team Deathmatch

Pure infantry combat. No vehicles, just skill versus skill in close-quarters battle arenas.

Hazard Zone

High-stakes squad-based mode. Complete objectives, extract safely, and survive against enemy squads.

Portal

Create and play custom game modes. Mix and match content from different Battlefield eras for endless possibilities.
